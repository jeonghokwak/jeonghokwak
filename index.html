    • Jeongho Kwak is currently an Assistant Professor at Department of Information and Communication Engineering, DGIST (Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology), Daegu, Korea. He is leading the Intelligent Computing & Networking Laboratory (ICNL) at DGIST. His research interests lie on compute/storage/network resource allocation in cloud computing and 5G network architecture with optimization and learning tools. He received the Master Degree and Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from KAIST, Daejeon, Korea, in Feb. 2011 and Feb. 2015, respectively. He received the Bachelor degree (Summa Cum Laude) in electrical engineering from Ajou University, Suwon, Korea in Aug. 2008.
